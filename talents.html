Talents

EMC offers strategic solutions tailored to the individual needs of every talent.

Annual

We work with a select number of talents around the year with a focus on building their fashion profile through elevated editorials.

Alexandra Pereira
Personable and elegant Alexandra Pereira has joined EMC since last Fall of 2020 and has built since then solid relationships with some of the most desired fashion houses including Dior, Balmain, Chloé, Hermès, Miu Miu, Fendi and Louis Vuitton.

alexandrapereira

Allegra Shaw
Allegra’s youthful energy and aesthetic are rapidly conquering the Canadian market from Toronto where she is based. She is a savvy entrepreneur / It-Girl with a flourishing clothing brand, as well as successful Youtuber, boasting a fast-growing community of engaged followers on all of her platforms.

allegrashaw

Brittany Xavier
Brittany Xavier has been part of the EMC Community since 2017. In record time, Brittany transformed into a Social Media sensation and Fashion It Girl who works regularly with Marc Jacobs, Dior, Louis Vuitton, Fendi, Versace, Chanel and many more.

brittanyxavier

Chriselle Lim
Chriselle Lim was the first influencer to sign on with EMC back in 2016 and the journey has been one that has taken this elegant social media pioneer, entrepreneur and Mom from the front row at Paris Fashion Week to the red carpet of Cannes and around the globe.

chrisellelim

Coco Bassey
Since she started off seasonally with EMC, Coco Bassey has now firmly established her warm and minimalist aesthetic, working on beautiful editorials with brands including Fendi, Chanel, Koché, Patou and Tod’s.

cocobassey

Jessica Wang
Jessica Wang is a Chinese American influencer known for her creative direction and passion for empowering women around the globe. She has caught the attention of Louis Vuitton, Lanvin, Schiaparelli, Mugler, Isabel Marant, Balmain, Giambattista Valli, Alaïa and more.

jessicawang

Sai de Silva
Sai de Silva is on her way to conquer the luxury world with the sunniest energy! This unapologetically glamourous Brooklyn Mom has already caught the eye of Alaïa, Dior and Jean-Paul Gaultier and just came back from Paris where she had a beautiful fashion week.

scoutthecity

Sira Pevida
Sira’s audience flocks to her profiles for her whimsical and top-brand content that transcends the simple post and reaches the production and storytelling of magazine editorials. This Mexican socialite who has always had a strong following throughout Latin America and recently spread into the US since making Miami her home.

sirapevida

Viky Rader
Proving that elegance is not only skin deep, the Munich based Ukrainian has recently dedicated much her time to her passion project VR_wearetogether, a NGO creating homes for displaced Ukrainians

vikyandthekid
