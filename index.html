Expertise

European Fashion Weeks

Based on inside-out knowledge of the industry and of the European fashion capitals, we take care of logistics for the Ready to Wear and Haute Couture shows in London, Milan and Paris: securing invitations to shows and events, arranging interviews and photo coverage of designer showrooms, and coordinating local accommodation and transportation. We also operate as the on-site facilitator for our clients’ various Fashion Week operations and events. Year-round, we develop and maintain relationships with fashion houses, PRs, advertisers, and all other players in the fashion and luxury industries.

Digital Services

We bring the same expertise to our portfolio of carefully selected digital influencers, assisting them navigate Fashion Weeks by introducing them to houses relevant to their brand, scheduling meetings and making connections, securing seats at shows, and opening doors for future editorials and collaborations. Annual contracts focus on image building, relationship networking and creative communication, facilitating organic brand development in luxury markets even further.
